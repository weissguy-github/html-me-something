Meet the Granddog Reasons to Adopt

Lost and Found

In February of 2014, my son-in-law rescued a young Pit Bull from the cold streets of North St Louis city. When told, my wife and I warned our daughter and son-in-law about the liability and danger of having one of those ferocious beasts in their house. Needless to say, they didn't listen.

Even her muscles have muscles

It's worse than we thought. What we failed to realize at the time was not only was this beast going to be in their house, but the beast would spend time at our house. We were now grandparents of an approximately two year old 65 lb. pile of bones, teeth, and muscle Staffordshire Terrier mix, aka Pit Bull.

People puppy

Lucky(?) for us, while strong as a bull and quite rambunctious in her early years, she is a people person through and through. She sometimes even sits like people.

Plenty of exercise is key

Tired dogs are less likely to misbehave, especially young high energy dogs.

And of course, they're all angels when sleeping

She's a hoot, even when she sleeps

But, sometimes, maybe a cat would be better

Because ... SHE. CAN. WEAR. YOU. DOWN.

Velcro dog

If you talk to owners of Pitties, you will find a common thread. Deeply loyal, always at your side, on your side, against your side, in your lap... they are like velcro. If you sit ...

...or lie down, she will be on you or leaning against you one way or another. Yes, it can be painful, but I have to admit it is kind of nice, too.

A word to the wise...

Training is key... and keep training. Chloe is very bright, and strong willed. We were unable to break her of sleeping on the bed and getting on the furniture. She is not at our house long enough to train her on those things and that behavior is allowed at her home. So when she visits, the furniture is covered.

... and the old sheets go on the bed.

REASONS TO ADOPT

So... if you are looking for a great companion

Someone who is a good listener

Someone who will stay by your side

Someone who will watch over your house

Someone who will laugh at your jokes

... or not

and... someone who will bring a smile to your face

Go to a shelter and ADOPT! It will warm your heart.
