Chloe's story

Lost and Found

In February of 2014, we were told my son-in-law rescued a young Pit Bull from the cold streets of North St Louis city. My wife and I warned our daughter about the liability and danger of having one of those ferocious beasts in their house.

Even her muscles have muscles

What we failed to realize at the time, was just how much time the beast would spend at our house. We were now grandparents of an approximately two year old 65 lb. pile of bones, teeth, and muscle Staffordshire Terrier mix, aka Pit Bull.

She even sits like a person

Lucky(?) for us, while strong as a bull and quite rambunctious in her early years, she was a people person through and through.

Plenty of exercise is key

Tired dogs are less likely to misbehave, especially young high energy dogs.

And of course, they're all angels when sleeping

She's a hoot, even when she sleeps

But, sometimes, maybe a cat would be better

Because ... SHE. CAN. WEAR. YOU. DOWN.

Velcro dog

If you talk to owners of Pitties, you will find a common thread. Deeply loyal, always at you side, on your side, against your side, in your lap... they are like velcro. If you sit ...

...or lie down, she will be on you or leaning against you one way or another. Yes, it can be painful, but I have to admit it is kind of nice, too.

A word to the wise...

Training is key... and keep training. Chloe is very bright, and strong willed. We were unable to break her of sleeping on the bed and getting on the furniture because she is not at our house long enough to train her on those things. We give in to her since those things are allowed at her home. So now when she visits, the furniture is covered.

... and the old sheets go on the bed.

So... if you are looking for a great companion

Someone who is a good listener

Someone who will stay by your side

Someone who will watch over your house

Someone who will laugh at your jokes

and... someone who will bring a smile to your face

Go to a shelter and ADOPT!
